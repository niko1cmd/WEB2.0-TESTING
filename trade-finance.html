What Is Trade Finance?

Bridge the Gap Between
Invoice and Payment.

Trade finance is the capital that sits between paying your supplier and collecting from your customer. FinXtra pays your supplier immediately, in their currency, at a locked FX rate. You repay when your own receivable lands. Finance and FX, handled together, by one advisor.

Supplier Paid Immediately

FinXtra releases funds to your supplier the moment the facility is approved, in their currency, at a locked rate. No delays, no renegotiated supplier terms.

Repayment Aligned to Your Trade Cycle

Terms from 30 to 180 days, structured around when your customer actually settles. No mismatch between your receivable timeline and your repayment date.

FX Built Into Every Facility

Your supplier's currency conversion is part of the deal, locked at an institutional rate when the facility is confirmed. No separate FX step, no second margin layer.

One Advisor, Both Problems Solved

A named FinXtra advisor structures the finance and the FX together, with full transparency on total cost before you approve a single dollar.
