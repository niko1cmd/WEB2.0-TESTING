Africa Coverage

The Continent Where Most Providers Stop. We Don't.

Most FX providers handle the G10 currencies effortlessly and go quiet the moment you mention Naira, Birr, or West African CFA Franc. They'll tell you it's "complex." We'll tell you it's our specialty.

FinXtra has built direct corridors and compliance frameworks across Africa — including restricted-currency environments and managed-float markets that your bank won't touch without a 6-week onboarding process and a stack of unexplained fees.

  • NGN (Nigeria) — Direct transfers, fully documented for NGO compliance
  • KES (Kenya) — Fast disbursements to field operations across East Africa
  • ETB (Ethiopia) — Regulated structure for restricted-currency environments
  • XOF / XAF (West & Central Africa) — Full WAEMU & CEMAC zone coverage
  • GHS, TZS, UGX, RWF — Direct settlement, no hidden correspondent fees
  • ZAR + SADC corridor — Southern Africa coverage from South Africa outward

Latin America Coverage

We've Built Around It —
Because Volatility Is the Region's Reality.

Latin America is one of the fastest-growing regions for international NGO activity — and one of the most underserved by conventional FX providers. IOF taxes in Brazil, SPEI integration in Mexico, managed-exchange environments in Argentina — these aren't edge cases. They're the reality of operating in the region.

FinXtra has built market-specific compliance frameworks for each country — so your team moves funds with confidence, not workarounds.

  • BRL (Brazil) — IOF-compliant transfer structure for registered NGOs
  • MXN (Mexico) — SPEI-integrated, same-day settlement to beneficiaries
  • COP (Colombia) — Direct settlement, no unofficial channel exposure
  • PEN (Peru) & CLP (Chile) — Andean corridor with full documentation
  • ARS (Argentina) — Compliant managed-exchange framework for NGO operations
