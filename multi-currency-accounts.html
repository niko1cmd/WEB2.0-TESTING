What Are Collection Accounts?

Your Own Account.
In Every Currency You Need.

Multi-currency collection accounts are dedicated accounts under your company name — one for each currency you choose. Your clients send payment domestically in their currency. Funds land in your account and stay there until you decide to convert, pay out, or hold.

Accounts in Your Name

Each account is registered to your business — not a shared pool. Your clients see your company name when they pay, building trust and clarity.

Receive Like a Local

Give clients local payment details for their currency. No international wire fees on their end — just a simple domestic transfer that arrives faster.

Convert on Your Terms

Hold balances until the rate suits you. Convert into 135+ currencies or pay suppliers directly — no forced timing, no bank pressure.

Full Transaction Records

Every receipt, conversion, and outgoing payment is logged and exportable — clean for accounting, audit, and reconciliation.
