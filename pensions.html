Why It Matters

Banks Quietly Take a Percentage of Every Inheritance.

Most people never realize it. When a foreign estate is settled and funds are wired to Canada, the bank applies a retail FX margin of 2–4% before the money reaches your account. On a £200,000 inheritance, that's £4,000–£8,000 that simply disappears. FinXtra quotes from the interbank market, shows you the full cost upfront, and gets you significantly more.

The same applies to foreign pension income. If you receive a UK pension, a US retirement distribution, or any recurring income in a foreign currency, every conversion at a bank retail rate compounds over time. A dedicated FinXtra advisor sets up a plan that protects your income at every payment cycle.
