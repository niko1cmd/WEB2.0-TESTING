The FX Dimension of Legal Practice

Currency Risk Is in Every Cross-Border File.

Legal professionals handle some of the most time-sensitive, high-value financial transactions in any industry. Settlement payments, retainer transfers, immigration funding, M&A closings, and estate distributions all carry FX exposure that most firms leave unmanaged. That gap is a risk to your clients, and an opportunity for your practice.

Settlement & Trust Account Transfers

Cross-border settlements and trust account payments are rate-sensitive and time-critical. An unfavorable rate or a delayed wire on a closing day is more than a financial issue it's a professional one.

Immigration & Relocation Funding

Immigration clients need to move funds internationally proof of funds, settlement capital, visa deposits. The timing and rate on these transfers can make or break an application.

M&A, Corporate & Estate Transactions

Multi-jurisdictional corporate transactions, cross-border estate distributions, and international M&A closings all carry embedded FX exposure that requires expert management.
