The FX Dimension of Wealth Management

Every Global Portfolio Carries Currency Risk. Most of It Is Unmanaged.

Your clients' wealth doesn't sit in one currency. Foreign property, international equities, offshore income, cross-border estate distributions every one of these carries FX exposure that directly impacts net returns. The question isn't whether currency risk exists. It's whether anyone is actively managing it. With FinXtra, the answer is yes and that answer comes from you.

Erosion of Returns at Bank Rates

High-net-worth clients moving significant sums through their bank's FX desk lose 1–3% to institutional margins on every conversion compounding across multiple transactions and jurisdictions to materially impact real returns.

Unhedged Portfolio Currency Exposure

Foreign property values, offshore investment income, and international distributions are all exposed to rate fluctuations. Without a hedging strategy, currency becomes an uncontrolled variable in your client's financial plan.

Multi-Generational Wealth Transfers

Family offices managing cross-border estate distributions, trust transfers, and inter-generational wealth movements require precision FX execution rates and timing that a bank's standard process cannot reliably deliver.
