FAQ

Common Questions About Payments

A spot payment converts and settles at the current live market rate, typically within the same business day or the next. A forward contract lets you lock in a rate today for a payment that will be made at a future date, up to 5 years ahead. Spot is ideal for immediate transfers; forwards protect you against future rate movements.

There is no hard limit on the number of recipients in a bulk batch. Clients regularly run batches with hundreds of payees across multiple currencies in a single file upload. For very large or complex runs, your dedicated advisor can help structure the batch for optimal settlement timing.

Same-day settlement is available on major corridors including CAD, USD, EUR, GBP, and MXN when payments are booked before the daily cut-off time. Emerging market currencies typically settle within 1–2 business days depending on local banking infrastructure. Your advisor will confirm timing for your specific corridor.

FinXtra earns on the FX spread, which is disclosed to you before every transaction. There are no hidden fees added after confirmation, no monthly account fees, and no minimum transaction requirements. What you see at confirmation is what you pay.
